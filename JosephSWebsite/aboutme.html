Hello everyone. My name is Joseph Sangurima and I'm an animation student.
My favorite things to do are drawing, listening to music, browsing around the internet and playing videogames.
What inspired me to follow animation is kind of hard to say. I know that ever since I was little I loved watching cartoons, but that was the extent of it. Usually I would be obsessing over the latest Spider-Man movie or playing video games. It could stem from there though since most of what I saw had to deal with the extraordinary, and animation does offer limitless possibilities in terms of what you can do.
Some of my biggest influences are from 2D animation, most seen in a majority of cartoons, superhero comics, and anime. Just the way the animators makes the characters move and react is something breathtaking and brings a feeling of realism that, quite ironically, real life can't imitate.
